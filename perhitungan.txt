FINSIGHT SYSTEM GUIDANCE
Financial Health Evaluation & Consumptive Behavior Analysis

========================================================
1. SYSTEM OVERVIEW
========================================================

FinSight adalah sistem keuangan pribadi berbasis web yang bertujuan untuk:
- mencatat transaksi keuangan pengguna
- mengevaluasi kesehatan finansial
- menganalisis perilaku konsumtif
- memberikan insight otomatis
- memberikan warning dan rekomendasi finansial

Metode evaluasi menggunakan:
COMPOSITE INDEX + MULTI INDICATOR SCORING + EQUAL WEIGHTING

Pendekatan ini mengacu pada:
- OECD Composite Indicator Framework
- Financial Well-being Framework
- Behavioral Finance Theory
- Consumer Behavior Theory

========================================================
2. FINANCIAL HEALTH FRAMEWORK
========================================================

Financial Health Score (FHS) dibangun dari 3 dimensi utama:

1. Objective Indicators (O)
   → kondisi finansial nyata

2. Behavioral Indicators (B)
   → perilaku pengeluaran dan konsumsi

3. Subjective Indicators (S)
   → persepsi dan rasa aman finansial pengguna

Seluruh indikator dinilai menggunakan:
- ordinal scoring
- normalisasi 0–100
- equal weighting

========================================================
3. OBJECTIVE INDICATORS
========================================================

Objective indicators berasal dari data transaksi pengguna.

--------------------------------------------------------
3.1 Saving Ratio
--------------------------------------------------------

Formula:
Saving Ratio = Total Savings / Total Income

Tujuan:
Mengukur kemampuan pengguna menyisihkan pendapatan.

Interpretasi:
- semakin tinggi semakin baik
- menunjukkan financial sustainability

--------------------------------------------------------
3.2 Expense Ratio
--------------------------------------------------------

Formula:
Expense Ratio = Total Expense / Total Income

Tujuan:
Mengukur kontrol pengeluaran.

Interpretasi:
- semakin rendah semakin baik
- expense terlalu tinggi menunjukkan risiko finansial

--------------------------------------------------------
3.3 Cash Flow
--------------------------------------------------------

Formula:
Cash Flow = Total Income - Total Expense

Tujuan:
Mengukur kestabilan arus kas.

Interpretasi:
- positif = sehat
- negatif = defisit

--------------------------------------------------------
3.4 Debt Ratio
--------------------------------------------------------

Formula:
Debt Ratio = Total Debt / Total Income

Tujuan:
Mengukur tekanan utang terhadap pendapatan.

Interpretasi:
- semakin rendah semakin baik

========================================================
4. BEHAVIORAL INDICATORS
========================================================

Behavioral indicators digunakan untuk menganalisis perilaku konsumtif pengguna.

--------------------------------------------------------
4.1 Consumptive Ratio
--------------------------------------------------------

Formula:
Consumptive Ratio = Consumptive Expense / Total Expense

Kategori konsumtif:
- hiburan
- shopping
- food delivery
- gadget
- lifestyle spending
- non-essential spending

Interpretasi:
- semakin tinggi semakin konsumtif

--------------------------------------------------------
4.2 Transaction Frequency
--------------------------------------------------------

Tujuan:
Mengukur intensitas transaksi.

Interpretasi:
- transaksi kecil berulang menunjukkan impulsive spending

--------------------------------------------------------
4.3 Impulsive Spending Pattern
--------------------------------------------------------

Deteksi:
- transaksi kecil namun sering
- transaksi mendadak
- dominasi kategori hiburan
- dominasi non-essential spending

Indikasi:
- impulsive buying behavior

--------------------------------------------------------
4.4 Budget Discipline
--------------------------------------------------------

Tujuan:
Mengukur kemampuan pengguna menjaga pengeluaran tetap sesuai batas.

Interpretasi:
- pengeluaran stabil = disiplin baik
- pengeluaran melebihi pemasukan = disiplin buruk

========================================================
5. SUBJECTIVE INDICATORS
========================================================

Subjective indicators diperoleh dari self-assessment bulanan pengguna.

Menggunakan skala Likert 1–5.

--------------------------------------------------------
5.1 Financial Satisfaction
--------------------------------------------------------

Pertanyaan contoh:
"Saya puas dengan kondisi keuangan saya saat ini."

--------------------------------------------------------
5.2 Financial Security
--------------------------------------------------------

Pertanyaan contoh:
"Saya merasa aman secara finansial."

--------------------------------------------------------
5.3 Financial Confidence
--------------------------------------------------------

Pertanyaan contoh:
"Saya percaya diri dalam mengelola keuangan."

========================================================
6. ORDINAL SCORING METHOD
========================================================

Semua indikator dikonversi menjadi skor ordinal.

--------------------------------------------------------
Scoring Scale
--------------------------------------------------------

5 = Sangat Baik
4 = Baik
3 = Cukup
2 = Buruk
1 = Sangat Buruk

========================================================
7. SCORE NORMALIZATION
========================================================

Final score dinormalisasi ke skala 0–100.

Formula:

Financial Health Score =
(Total Indicator Score / Maximum Score) × 100

========================================================
8. EQUAL WEIGHTING METHOD
========================================================

Setiap indikator diberikan bobot yang sama.

Alasan:
- tidak terdapat standar baku bobot indikator
- sesuai metode OECD composite index
- lebih stabil secara akademik
- lebih explainable untuk sistem evaluasi finansial

========================================================
9. FINAL HEALTH CATEGORY
========================================================

80 – 100
Status: Sangat Sehat

60 – 79
Status: Sehat

40 – 59
Status: Cukup

20 – 39
Status: Berisiko

0 – 19
Status: Tidak Sehat

========================================================
10. INSIGHT GENERATION ENGINE
========================================================

Sistem harus menghasilkan insight otomatis berdasarkan pola data.

--------------------------------------------------------
Contoh Insight
--------------------------------------------------------

- Pengeluaran Anda lebih besar dibanding pemasukan.
- Tabungan Anda masih rendah.
- Pengeluaran konsumtif cukup tinggi.
- Arus kas Anda negatif bulan ini.
- Pengeluaran hiburan meningkat dibanding bulan lalu.
- Anda sering melakukan transaksi kecil berulang.
- Pengeluaran non-esensial mendominasi pengeluaran bulanan.

========================================================
11. WARNING SYSTEM
========================================================

Warning diberikan jika kondisi finansial berisiko.

--------------------------------------------------------
Trigger Warning
--------------------------------------------------------

Saving Ratio < 10%
→ warning saving rendah

Expense Ratio > 90%
→ warning pengeluaran tinggi

Cash Flow negatif
→ warning defisit finansial

Consumptive Ratio > 70%
→ warning perilaku konsumtif tinggi

Debt Ratio tinggi
→ warning tekanan utang tinggi

========================================================
12. RECOMMENDATION ENGINE
========================================================

Sistem memberikan rekomendasi otomatis berdasarkan kondisi finansial.

--------------------------------------------------------
Contoh Recommendation
--------------------------------------------------------

- Kurangi pengeluaran hiburan.
- Tingkatkan tabungan minimal 10% dari pendapatan.
- Batasi transaksi impulsif.
- Buat anggaran bulanan.
- Prioritaskan kebutuhan dibanding keinginan.
- Kurangi penggunaan kredit konsumtif.
- Tingkatkan emergency fund.

========================================================
13. TREND ANALYSIS
========================================================

Sistem perlu melakukan analisis tren bulanan.

--------------------------------------------------------
Analisis yang disarankan
--------------------------------------------------------

- saving naik / turun
- expense naik / turun
- perubahan cash flow
- perubahan perilaku konsumtif
- perubahan kategori pengeluaran dominan

--------------------------------------------------------
Contoh Output
--------------------------------------------------------

- Pengeluaran meningkat 18% dibanding bulan lalu.
- Tabungan meningkat 12% dibanding bulan sebelumnya.
- Pengeluaran hiburan mengalami kenaikan signifikan.
- Kondisi finansial membaik dibanding periode sebelumnya.

========================================================
14. IMPLEMENTATION FLOW
========================================================

INPUT:
- income
- expense
- saving
- debt
- category
- transaction frequency
- subjective survey

PROCESS:
1. calculate financial ratios
2. convert to ordinal score
3. aggregate all indicators
4. normalize score
5. generate category
6. generate insights
7. generate warnings
8. generate recommendations

OUTPUT:
- financial health score
- health category
- consumptive analysis
- insights
- warnings
- recommendations

========================================================
15. SYSTEM PRINCIPLES
========================================================

FinSight harus:
- explainable
- transparent
- behavior-oriented
- easy to understand
- practical for users
- academically justifiable

Sistem tidak hanya fokus pada angka,
tetapi juga fokus pada perilaku finansial pengguna.
